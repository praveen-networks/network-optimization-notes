## 📶 Wi-Fi Optimization Basics

- Avoid DFS channels in high-density environments  
- Maintain proper channel separation  
- Prefer 5 GHz band for better performance  
- Monitor client load per access point  

---

## ⚠️ Common Issues

### Frequent Disconnections
- Check RF interference  
- Reduce channel overlap  
- Validate firmware stability  

### Slow Speeds
- Check client distribution  
- Verify backhaul capacity  
- Analyze signal strength

## ⚙️ Advanced Wi-Fi Tuning (Enterprise Scenario)

- Use 20 MHz channel width in high-density environments  
- Disable lower data rates to improve airtime efficiency  
- Optimize transmit power to avoid co-channel interference  
- Enable band steering to push clients to 5 GHz  
- Monitor AP utilization and rebalance clients  

---

## 📊 Real-World Observation

In high-density campus environments:

- Overlapping channels caused frequent disconnections  
- Reducing transmit power improved roaming stability  
- Proper channel planning significantly improved throughput
